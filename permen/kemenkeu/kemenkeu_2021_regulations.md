**TOTAL FOUND: 224 PMK REGULATIONS**

## MAJOR COVID-19 RESPONSE REGULATIONS 2021

### PMK 226/PMK.03/2021 - COVID Tax Incentives Extension
- **Title:** Pemberian Insentif Pajak terhadap Barang yang Diperlukan dalam Rangka Penanganan Pandemi Corona Virus Disease 2019 dan Perpanjangan Pemberlakuan Fasilitas
- **Date:** December 31, 2021
- **Theme:** Tax incentives for COVID goods + PEN program extension
- **Significance:** Final extension of COVID tax facilities

### PMK 189/PMK.06/2021 - PEN Investment Program Amendment  
- **Title:** Perubahan atas PMK 118/PMK.06/2020 tentang Investasi Pemerintah dalam rangka Program Pemulihan Ekonomi Nasional
- **Date:** December 16, 2021
- **Theme:** Government investment in National Economic Recovery
- **Significance:** Modified PEN investment framework

### PMK 184/PMK.05/2021 - COVID Budget Completion
- **Title:** Pelaksanaan Anggaran dalam rangka Penyelesaian Pekerjaan pada Masa Pandemi Corona Virus Disease 2019 yang Tidak Terselesaikan
- **Date:** December 14, 2021
- **Theme:** Budget execution for unfinished COVID work
- **Significance:** Budget flexibility for pandemic response completion

## STRATEGIC ANALYSIS 2021

**COVID RESPONSE EVOLUTION:**
- Transition from emergency response to recovery investment
- Budget flexibility maintained for pandemic completion
- Tax incentives extended but with clear end dates

**ECONOMIC RECOVERY FOCUS:**  
- Government investment program modifications
- Export financing institution strengthening
- Warehouse receipt subsidy introduction

**INSTITUTIONAL STRENGTHENING:**
- Risk management formalization
- Procurement procedure updates
- Enhanced revenue oversight systems

**DIGITAL ACCELERATION:**
- New accounting systems implementation
- Enhanced revenue monitoring mechanisms
