# Ministry of Finance - Ministerial Regulations

## Ministry Information
- **Full Name:** Kementerian Keuangan Republik Indonesia
- **Minister:** Sri Mulyani Indrawati
- **Established:** 1945
- **Headquarters:** Jakarta

## Regulatory Authority
The Ministry of Finance has authority over:
- National budget and fiscal policy
- Taxation and customs
- Government debt management
- Financial market oversight
- State asset management
- Regional fiscal policy

## Key Regulation Categories

### Taxation
- **Personal Income Tax (PPh)** - Individual and corporate tax regulations
- **Value Added Tax (PPN)** - VAT implementation and procedures
- **Land and Building Tax (PBB)** - Property taxation
- **Luxury Goods Sales Tax (PPnBM)** - Luxury tax regulations
- **Regional Taxes** - Local government tax coordination

### Customs and Excise
- **Import/Export Procedures** - Trade facilitation and customs processes
- **Tariff Classifications** - Harmonized system implementation
- **Excise Tax** - Excisable goods (tobacco, alcohol, etc.)
- **Free Trade Zone** - Special economic zone regulations
- **Anti-Dumping** - Trade defense mechanisms

### Government Finance
- **State Budget (APBN)** - Budget preparation and execution
- **Regional Finance** - Fiscal decentralization and transfer mechanisms
- **Government Borrowing** - Debt issuance and management
- **Cash Management** - Treasury and cash flow management
- **Financial Reporting** - Government financial accounting standards

### Financial Markets
- **Capital Markets** - Securities and investment regulations (coordination with OJK)
- **Insurance** - Insurance industry oversight (coordination with OJK)
- **Pension Funds** - Retirement and social security funds
- **State-Owned Enterprises Finance** - SOE financial management

## Implementation Statistics
- **Active Regulations:** ~500 total ministerial regulations
- **Annual Updates:** ~50-80 new/revised regulations per year
- **Sector Impact:** All economic sectors (universal tax/customs coverage)
- **Public Service:** Tax offices, customs offices, treasury services nationwide

## Digital Transformation Initiatives
- **Online Tax Filing** - Electronic tax return systems
- **Digital Customs** - Automated customs clearance
- **E-Billing** - Electronic tax payment systems  
- **Tax Compliance AI** - Automated compliance monitoring
- **Blockchain Integration** - Secure transaction verification

## Priority Regulation Areas (2024-2025)
1. **Digital Economy Taxation** - Comprehensive framework for digital business taxation
2. **Green Finance** - Environmental taxation and green bonds
3. **MSME Tax Incentives** - Small business tax facilitation
4. **Cross-Border Taxation** - International tax cooperation and BEPS implementation
5. **Financial Technology** - Fintech regulation and supervision

## Legal Framework Compliance
Ministry of Finance regulations implement:
- **UU No. 6/1983** - General Tax Provisions and Procedures (KUP)
- **UU No. 7/1983** - Income Tax (PPh)  
- **UU No. 8/1983** - Value Added Tax (PPN)
- **UU No. 17/2003** - State Finance
- **UU No. 1/2004** - State Treasury
