### 2. Personal Data Protection (PDP) Implementation
**[Perkomdigi No. 2 Tahun 2024](active-regulations.md#keamanan-siber)**

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Full Title** | Pelindungan Data Pribadi dalam Penyelenggaraan Sistem Elektronik |
| **Legal Basis** | [**UU 27/2022**](../../hierarchy/03-uu-perppu.md#uu-pdp) |
| **Effective Date** | 17 Oktober 2024 (2 years after PDP Law) |
| **Replaces** | [Permen 20/2016](change-history.md#2024-changes) |
| **Impact Scope** | ALL data controllers and processors |
| **Compliance Deadline** | 17 Oktober 2026 (full compliance) |

#### üéØ Why Critical?
- **GDPR-Level Impact:** Fundamental change in data processing landscape
- **Severe Penalties:** Up to Rp 50 billion fines + criminal liability
- **Universal Scope:** Affects every organization processing personal data
- **International Standards:** Aligns Indonesia with global data protection norms

#### üìä Key Provisions
- **Consent Mechanisms:** Explicit consent requirements
- **Data Subject Rights:** Access, rectification, deletion, portability
- **Cross-Border Transfers:** Adequacy requirements for international transfers
- **Breach Notification:** 72-hour notification requirement
- **Privacy Impact Assessments:** Mandatory for high-risk processing
