# Domain Update - Ministry Transformation

## Official Legal Database Domain

### **NEW: https://jdih.komdigi.go.id/**

The official legal information system (JDIH) for the Ministry of Communication and Digital has been updated to reflect the ministry's transformation from "Kominfo" to "Komdigi".

## Ministry Transformation Timeline

### 2024 and Earlier
- **Ministry Name:** Kementerian Komunikasi dan Informatika (Kominfo)
- **Regulation Prefix:** Permen Kominfo
- **Domain:** Previous government portals

### 2025 and Later  
- **Ministry Name:** Kementerian Komunikasi dan Digital (Komdigi)
- **Regulation Prefix:** Perkomdigi (Peraturan Menteri Komunikasi dan Digital)
- **Official Domain:** **https://jdih.komdigi.go.id/**

## JDIH System Features

The new legal information system provides:
- **Complete regulation database** - All ministerial regulations from 2020-present
- **Advanced search capabilities** - Search by year, number, topic, legal status
- **Document download** - Full PDF versions of all regulations
- **Legal status tracking** - Active, amended, or revoked regulation status
- **Cross-reference system** - Links between related regulations
- **Mobile-responsive design** - Accessible on all devices

## Integration with This Repository

This repository serves as a **supplementary resource** providing:
- **Markdown conversion** of regulations for AI analysis
- **Structured categorization** by impact level and business relevance  
- **Historical analysis** and regulation numbering explanations
