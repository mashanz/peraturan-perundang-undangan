# PP - Peraturan Pemerintah (Government Regulations)

## Metadata
- **Jenis:** Peraturan Pemerintah (PP)
- **Hierarki:** Level 4
- **Lembaga Pembentuk:** Pemerintah/Presiden
- **Dasar Hukum:** Pasal 5 ayat (2) UUD 1945

## Function
Government Regulations are issued to implement laws (UU) and provide detailed operational guidelines for law enforcement and implementation.

## Document Structure
Each PP typically contains:
- **Menimbang** - Consideration basis
- **Mengingat** - Legal references
- **Memutuskan** - Decision to establish
- **Chapters and Articles** - Detailed regulations
- **Implementation provisions** - Execution guidelines
- **Transitional provisions** - Change management
- **Final provisions** - Effectiveness date

## Legal Status
- **Berlaku** - Currently active
- **Dicabut** - Revoked by newer PP
- **Diubah** - Amended by newer PP
- **Diganti** - Replaced by newer PP
