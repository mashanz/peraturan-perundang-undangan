# Perpres - Peraturan Presiden (Presidential Regulations)

## Metadata
- **Jenis:** Peraturan Presiden (Perpres)
- **Hierarki:** Level 5
- **Lembaga Pembentuk:** Presiden
- **Dasar Hukum:** Pasal 4 ayat (1) UUD 1945

## Function
Presidential Regulations are issued by the President to regulate matters under executive authority and provide operational details for government policies.

## Document Structure
Each Perpres typically contains:
- **Menimbang** - Consideration basis
- **Mengingat** - Legal references
- **Memutuskan** - Presidential decision
- **Chapters and Articles** - Regulatory provisions
- **Implementation guidelines** - Execution procedures
- **Final provisions** - Effectiveness and transition

## Legal Authority
Presidential Regulations are issued based on:
- Constitutional authority of the President
- Delegation from laws (UU)
- Implementation of government policies
- Administrative necessity for executive functions

## Legal Status
- **Berlaku** - Currently active
- **Dicabut** - Revoked by newer Perpres
- **Diubah** - Amended by newer Perpres
- **Diganti** - Replaced by newer Perpres
