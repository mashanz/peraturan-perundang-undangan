## Document Structure
Each law contains:
- **Pembukaan** - Preamble with legal basis
- **Batang Tubuh** - Main articles organized by chapters
- **Penutup** - Closing provisions
- **Penjelasan** - Explanatory notes
- **Lampiran** - Attachments (if any)

## Legal Status Categories
- **Berlaku** - Currently active
- **Dicabut** - Revoked by newer legislation
- **Diubah** - Amended by newer legislation
- **Diganti** - Replaced by newer legislation
